About Servant Summit

Founded on the principles of love, service, and community, Servant Summit Church has been a beacon of hope and faith for over two decades.

Our Mission

To create a loving community where people can encounter God, grow in their faith, and serve others with compassion. We believe in the transformative power of God's love and strive to be His hands and feet in our community.

Our Values

Faith

Deepening our relationship with God through prayer, worship, and study.

Community

Building authentic relationships and supporting one another.

Service

Serving our neighbors and making a positive impact in the world.

Grace

Extending God's love and forgiveness to all people.
